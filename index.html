I am a Software Developer at Helpshift, a San Francisco startup that builds SDKs for customer relationship management inside mobile apps. At Helpshift, I primarily work with Clojure in the back-end. I am a Computer Science graduate (MS) from University of Nice, France and St. Xavier's College, Kolkata (BSc). As a student I have done many projects mostly with Java, Python, PHP and Matlab. A list of my other projects can be found here. For my master thesis, I worked as a graduate research intern on finding spam on Twitter at Inria, France. This project was jointly funded by Inria and Microsoft Research.

Apart from Software Development, I am a believer in social networks and data science. I love to work on my personal software development projects during my free time. They include a full-fledged social network built for education, Edusoc and a Java library for accessing the Twitter REST API.

I love traveling, listening to music, and watching popular TV shows.
