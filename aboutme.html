About me

I was born and brought up in Kolkata, India. I studied at St. Xavier's Collegiate School. I did my undergraduate studies from St Xavier's College, Kolkata in Computer Science for three years. Then I went to France to do my masters in Computer Science at University of Nice Sophia Antipolis.

I am interested in research and development in Computer Science. I have always aspired to work as a Software Developer. I want to work on projects that have a real world significance, and can be used for the greater good of mankind.

Interests

I love to listen to music. I also play the keyboard. I am an amateur photographer and I love to take photographs. I have taken part in a couple of photography competitions during my undergraduate study. I also like playing football - I used to be in the departmental football team at St Xavier's College.

Places visited

I have been to many places in India like Delhi, Darjeeling, Goa, Lava. I have also been lucky to have visited European countries like Germany, Luxembourg, Switzerland. But to this day, two of my most favourite places are Darjeeling and Kolkata! I love to travel with friends and family whenever I get the chance.
