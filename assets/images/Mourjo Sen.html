Mourjo Sen

I am a grad student in Computer Science at University of Nice Sophia Antipolis and I am working as a graduate research intern on the analysis of trust on Twitter at Inria, France. The six-month internship is jointly funded by Inria and Microsoft Research.

I am a software developer, data scientist, believer in social networks. I have worked in a number of software projects during my MS and BSc. My projects include a full-fledged social network built for education called "Edusoc". I have also worked on analyzing Twitter to find trustworthy users on Twitter under Arnaud Legout, Inria and Maksym Gabielkov, Inria. A list of my other projects can be found here.

I love traveling, listening to music, and watching popular TV shows.
